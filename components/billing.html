Billing Policy

Modest Designs

Last updated: September 25, 2025

This Billing Policy outlines the terms and conditions related to payments, invoicing, and billing for website design and related services provided by Modest Designs. By engaging our services, you agree to comply with this policy.

  1. Payment Terms

    • All projects require an initial deposit before work commences, as specified in your project agreement or invoice.
    • The remaining balance is due upon completion of the project and prior to the delivery of final files or website launch.
    • Payment methods accepted include EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer), credit/debit card, and other methods as agreed upon.

  2. Invoicing

    • Invoices will be issued for all services rendered, detailing the scope of work, payment schedule, and due dates.
    • Invoices are sent via email to the address provided by the client.

  3. Late Payments

    • Payments not received by the due date may incur a late fee as specified in your agreement.
    • Modest Designs reserves the right to suspend work or withhold deliverables until outstanding payments are received.

  4. Refunds

    Refunds are subject to our Refund Policy. Please review the policy for details on eligibility and process.

  5. Disputes

    If you believe there is an error in your invoice or have a billing dispute, please contact us promptly at info@modestdesigns.co.za for resolution.

  6. Contact Information

    If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Billing Policy, please contact us at info@modestdesigns.co.za.
